### **Carnivore Sous Vide Recipe: Sous Vide Ribeye Steak with Herb Butter**

#### **Ingredients:**
- 1 ribeye steak (10-12 oz)
- 2 tbsp butter (for searing)
- Sea salt (to taste)
- Optional: fresh herbs like thyme or rosemary

#### **Prep & Cook Time:**
- **Prep time**: 5 minutes
- **Cook time**: 1.5-2 hours (sous vide) + 5 minutes (searing)
- **Total time**: 2 hours 10 minutes

#### **Instructions:**

1. **Prepare the Steak:**
   - Season the ribeye steak generously with sea salt.
   - Place the steak in a vacuum-sealed bag or a resealable plastic bag, making sure to remove as much air as possible.

2. **Sous Vide Cooking:**
   - Preheat your sous vide water bath to **130°F (54°C)** for medium-rare.
   - Submerge the sealed steak in the water bath and cook for **1.5-2 hours**.

3. **Sear the Steak:**
   - Remove the steak from the bag and pat it dry.
   - Heat a skillet over high heat and add 2 tablespoons of butter.
   - Sear the steak for **1 minute per side** until a golden-brown crust forms.
   - Optionally, add fresh herbs during the searing process for extra flavor.

4. **Serve:**
   - Let the steak rest for 5 minutes before slicing and serving.

#### **Servings:**
- **1 serving**

#### **Nutritional Information (Per Serving)**:
- **Calories**: ~700-800 kcal
- **Protein**: ~50-55 grams
- **Fat**: ~55-60 grams
- **Carbs**: 0 grams (carnivore-friendly!)
