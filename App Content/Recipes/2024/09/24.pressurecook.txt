### **Carnivore Pressure Cook Recipe: Pressure-Cooked Pork Shoulder**

#### **Ingredients:**
- 2 lbs pork shoulder (cut into chunks)
- 2 tbsp tallow or ghee (for searing)
- Sea salt (to taste)
- Optional: fresh herbs like thyme or rosemary

#### **Prep & Cook Time:**
- **Prep time**: 10 minutes
- **Cook time**: 1 hour (pressure cooker) + 10 minutes for pressure release
- **Total time**: 1 hour 20 minutes

#### **Instructions:**

1. **Sear the Pork:**
   - Set your pressure cooker to the saut√© function.
   - Add tallow or ghee and sear the pork shoulder chunks for **2-3 minutes** on each side until browned.
   - Season generously with sea salt.

2. **Pressure Cook:**
   - After searing, add optional fresh herbs for extra flavor.
   - Lock the lid and cook on high pressure for **1 hour**.
   - Allow the pressure to release naturally for 10 minutes before manually releasing any remaining pressure.

3. **Serve:**
   - Remove the pork shoulder from the pressure cooker and let it rest for a few minutes before serving.

#### **Servings:**
- **4 servings**

- **Calories**: ~500-600 kcal
- **Protein**: ~40 grams
- **Fat**: ~40 grams
- **Carbs**: 0 grams (carnivore-friendly!)
